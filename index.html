About UBRAIN

UBRAIN is a research initiative developed to address the lack of reliable sub-daily rainfall information in ungauged and data-scarce river basins. The platform integrates satellite rainfall products, ground observations, bias correction, temporal disaggregation, IDF analysis, and hydrological modelling.

Sub-Daily Rainfall

Generation of 30-minute rainfall from daily satellite rainfall datasets.

IDF Curves

Design rainfall estimation for multiple durations and return periods.

Hydrological Modelling

Support for SWAT, HEC-HMS and peak discharge studies.
